About

Who We Are

Rooted in Rockland County, D’Alleva Lawn Care reflects a journey from a high school lawn care hobby to a professional, licensed, and insured landscaping service. My dedication to this craft ensures that every project, large or small, is handled with utmost precision and care. With a focus on quality, dependability, and meticulous attention to detail, I aim to bring out the best in Rockland County's landscapes, one yard at a time.

  • Dedicated to safety in every aspect of service.
